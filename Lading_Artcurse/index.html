WHAT CAN EXPECT US DURING THE TRAINING?

Mastering the basic principles of drawing: studying line, shape, composition and proportions.
Experimenting with different techniques: from monochrome pencil drawing to colored mediums such as watercolor or oil.
Practice working with various materials and tools: from ordinary pencils to specialized art brushes.

Who is Yoneyama Mai?

Yoneyama Mai is a Japanese artist known for her vibrant and creative works in the anime and manga genre.

She is known for her unique style and ability to create compelling and expressive characters. Her work is often characterized by a vibrant color palette, dynamic poses and emotional expression.

Mai Yoneyama has wide popularity in the anime fan community and continues to delight her fans with new works.

WHAT WILL YOU BE able to do at the end of your studies?

Master the basics of drawing: perspective, composition, light and shade and proportions.
Understand different drawing styles, including anime, realism, comics and others.
Be able to create expressive and proportional characters, objects and landscapes.
Understand the basic principles of color harmony and use them in your work.
Understand the process of creating animation if the course includes this aspect.
